Healing from rigid religious sexual messaging isn't about throwing everything away—it's about reclaiming your agency. By evaluating these scripts, you move from a place of fear and compliance to a place of choice and authentic embodiment.

Clinical Application

In a therapeutic setting, this exercise externalizes internalized shame, allowing the client to view their deeply held beliefs objectively. It utilizes cognitive restructuring while validating the protective nature of their original "baggage."
